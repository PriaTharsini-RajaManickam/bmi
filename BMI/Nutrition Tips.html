Nutrition Is The Key
Carbohydrates Vitamins and minerals Protein Water
Carbohydrates
These are your body's primary energy source, especially during intense workouts. Complex carbs like whole grains, fruits, and vegetables provide long-lasting energy and help maintain blood sugar levels High protein, High Fibers, Low carbs with healthy fats.
Vitamins and Minerals
Micronutrients like vitamin C, vitamin D, calcium, and iron are essential for bone health, immune function, and energy production. They help in recovery, preventing injury, and supporting metabolic functions. For example, vitamin D helps with calcium absorption, supporting bone density. High protein, High carbs with moderate healthy fats.
Protein
Essential for muscle growth and repair, protein is vital after workouts to repair tissue and promote recovery. Sources like lean meats, eggs, legumes, and plant-based proteins are important for building muscle and supporting overall health High protein, High Fibers, Low carbs with healthy fats.
Water
Hydration is often overlooked but is key to overall performance. Proper hydration ensures efficient digestion, optimal muscle function, and prevents fatigue during workouts. Drinking enough water is crucial for nutrient absorption and detoxification High protein, High carbs with moderate healthy fats.
